Most Special Moments From my Mission

On the mission I had many Spiritual moments. One of the most significant of such experiences was meeting Fernando and Romina, a couple that soon became very close to me and important people in my life. They had recently lost their baby girl and were searching for answers. We helped them understadn the importance of the plan of salvation and marriage and how one day if they made and kept covenants with God they could be reunited with their passed daughter forever in the presence of God. The spirit was so strong as we taught them and to this day they are active in the church.

Here is a video that we showed them about temples and families. See the youtube video by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Another special moment in my mission was when Argentina won the World Cup! Everyone wwas so emotional and excited and I felt as if I myself was an Argentine and that my own country won the World Cup.
